About The Show

The Second Year Show is a musical revue directed, written and produced entirely by the YSM Class of 2018. The show allows students to affectionately poke fun at faculty, peers and the medical student experience. The approximate running time of the show will be 2 hours, including an intermission. While the title and subject of the show are typically not released until a week before, please enjoy the following trailers!
